Class2Calendar

Seamlessly sync your Canvas LMS schedule with Google Calendar and Google Tasks.

About the Application

Class2Calendar is a Chrome extension designed to help students manage their academic schedule by syncing assignments and events directly from Canvas to Google's ecosystem.

📅 Calendar Sync

Automatically turn assignments into calendar events with due dates and times.

✅ Google Tasks

Create actionable Google Tasks for every assignment, complete with descriptions.

🔄 Auto-Updates

Changes in Canvas (like new due dates) are automatically reflected in your calendar.
